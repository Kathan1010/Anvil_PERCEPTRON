How KAVACH Works

Six autonomous agents work in concert to detect, investigate, and neutralize threats — from alert to resolution in seconds.

01. Alert Triage

SENTINEL detects anomalous behavior and classifies severity in real-time using ML models.

02. Enrichment

Parallel queries to VirusTotal, AbuseIPDB, NVD, and threat intel feeds gather deep context.

03. Investigation

SHERLOCK correlates evidence, maps MITRE ATT&CK techniques, and computes confidence scores.

04. Decision

High-confidence threats auto-remediate. Lower confidence triggers human-in-the-loop approval.

05. Remediation

DOCTOR executes containment — blocking IPs, alerting Slack, creating Jira tickets automatically.

06. Reporting

SCRIBE generates full incident reports and updates the knowledge graph for future correlations.
